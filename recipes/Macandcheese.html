Mac and Cheese

Desscription:

Mac and cheese is a delicious meal made from macaroni and cheese sauce. It is often baked with cheese and bread crumbs on top

ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Shredded cheese
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta
  2. Combine cheese and milk in pan until melted to sauce consistency
  3. Mix sauce with pasta in caserole dish
  4. Top with shredded cheese and bread crumbs
  5. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes, or until cheese is golden brown
