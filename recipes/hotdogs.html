Hot Dogs

Desscription:

Hot dogs are a staple of American food. They are often served with many condiments.

ingredients

  • Hot dogs
  • Buns
  • Condiments of your preference

Steps

  1. Grill hot dogs until cooked all the way through and have slight charing
  2. Toast buns on grill until warm and grill marks are visible
  3. Add hot dog to bun and top with condiments of choice
