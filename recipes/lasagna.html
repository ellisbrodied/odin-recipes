Lasagna

Desscription:

Lasagna is a pasta dish made from layering and baking pasta, meat, tomato sauce and cheese.

ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta
  2. Cook ground beef
  3. Layer pasta, ground beef, tomato sauce in a caserole dish until full
  4. Top with cheese
  5. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes, or until cheese is golden brown
